Shopify Product Images: WebP & Compression

Faster storefronts for Indian D2C brands — smaller files, same visual quality.

Why Shopify sellers optimize images

Heavy product photos slow mobile checkout and hurt SEO. WebP plus smart compression often cuts size 40–70% vs unoptimized JPG.

Recommended pipeline

  1. Resize hero images to your theme’s display width (often 1200–2000px max).
  2. Convert to WebP for modern browsers (keep JPG fallback if your theme requires).
  3. Compress before upload to Shopify Admin → Products.

Theme tips

  • Lazy-load galleries (most themes do this by default)
  • Avoid 4000px uploads when theme shows 800px
  • Use consistent aspect ratio across collection pages
