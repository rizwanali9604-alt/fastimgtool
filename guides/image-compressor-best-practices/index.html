Step-by-step workflow

  1. Open the tool and prepare your source file: Navigate to /tools/image-compressor/ in Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Use the original photo from your camera or supplier—not a forwarded chat image. Note the starting file size; Meesho listings perform best under 50KB and Amazon accepts up to 10MB but prefers around 2MB.
  2. Upload or drag your image into the workspace: Click the upload zone or drag a JPG, PNG, or WebP file directly onto the page. The Image Compressor preview loads instantly without creating an account.
  3. Adjust settings for your target platform: For Meesho, aim for 600×600 px square crops. For Amazon India, keep the longest edge at 1000–2000 px. Match white backgrounds (#FFFFFF) when marketplaces require clean catalog shots.
  4. Preview at 100% zoom before exporting: Check labels, stitching, and edges. Zoom to actual pixels—thumbnails hide blur that buyers notice on product pages.
  5. Download and verify the exported file: Save the processed image, confirm dimensions in file properties, and re-compress if still above 2MB. Upload a test SKU in Seller Central or Meesho panel before batch-processing your entire catalog.

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Starting from over-compressed sources: You cannot recover detail lost in a 30KB WhatsApp image. Always keep a full-resolution master and export marketplace copies with Image Compressor.
  • Ignoring platform dimension rules: Uploading 4000×3000 px photos when Meesho only needs 600×600 wastes bandwidth and triggers automatic downscaling that softens edges.
  • Applying maximum effect settings: Sliders at 100% rarely look professional. Subtle adjustments between 10–30% usually outperform dramatic edits on product photography.
  • Skipping a final compression pass: Even perfectly edited PNGs can exceed 2MB. Pair your workflow with Image Compressor before hitting upload on Amazon Seller Central.

Pro tips for Indian sellers

  • Shoot in daylight or use a ₹1,500 LED ring light—editing cannot fix extreme underexposure.
  • Keep masters in a /originals folder; export /marketplace copies at platform specs.
  • Batch similar SKUs together so Image Compressor settings stay consistent.
  • After editing, always pass through Image Compressor if files exceed 2MB.
  • Re-audit listings quarterly—Meesho and Amazon update image guidelines without loud announcements.
