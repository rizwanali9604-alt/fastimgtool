How to Resize an Image Online

Resizing images is essential for websites, social media, and email. Our free online resizer makes it instant and private.

Why Resize Images?

  • Optimize for web: Smaller images load faster.
  • Social media: Each platform has ideal dimensions.
  • Email attachments: Reduce file size to meet limits.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Go to the Image Resizer tool.
  2. Click 'Choose File' and select an image.
  3. Enter desired width and height (lock aspect ratio optional).
  4. Click 'Download' – your resized image is ready.
