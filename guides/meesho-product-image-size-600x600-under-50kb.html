Meesho Product Image: 600×600 px Under 50KB

Step-by-step for Indian Meesho sellers — resize and compress in your browser before upload.

Why Meesho sellers need 600×600 and small file size

Listing photos that are too large slow uploads and may fail on mobile networks. A square 600×600 px image under roughly 50KB keeps catalogs fast and consistent.

Step 1 — Resize to 600×600

  1. Open the Image Resizer.
  2. Upload your product photo.
  3. Set width and height to 600 (lock aspect ratio or crop to square first).
  4. Download the resized JPG or WebP.

Step 2 — Compress under 50KB

  1. Open the Image Compressor.
  2. Upload the 600×600 file.
  3. Lower quality until file size is under 50KB (usually 70–85% for product shots).
  4. Upload to your Meesho catalog.

Common mistakes

  • Uploading 3000px phone photos without resize
  • Heavy PNG backgrounds — use JPG for catalog
  • Blurry upscale — start from a sharp source image

Batch many SKUs?

Process one hero image per SKU this way; for large catalogs, repeat or bookmark this guide for your team.
