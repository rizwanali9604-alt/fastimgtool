Why Convert WebP to JPG?

Before diving into the steps, let’s explore why you might need to convert WebP to JPG:

  • Editing Flexibility: Some image editing tools work better with JPG files, giving you more options for customization.
  • Reduced Complexity: Converting WebP to JPG can simplify workflows, especially when dealing with older systems or software.
