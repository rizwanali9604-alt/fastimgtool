Why Convert HEIC to JPG?

HEIC (High-Efficiency Image Format) is a modern image format developed by Apple, offering high-quality images with smaller file sizes. However, its compatibility is limited, especially on non-Apple devices and platforms. JPG, on the other hand, is a universally supported format that works seamlessly across devices and applications. Converting HEIC to JPG ensures that your images are accessible and easy to share with anyone, regardless of their device or software.
