What Does It Mean to Invert Image Colors?

Inverting image colors means reversing the color values of an image, turning dark areas into light ones and light areas into dark ones. For example, black becomes white, blue becomes orange, and green becomes magenta. This process creates a "negative" version of the original image, which can be used for artistic purposes, scientific applications, or to make images more accessible to people with visual impairments.
