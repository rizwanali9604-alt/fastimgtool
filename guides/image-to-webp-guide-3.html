Introduction: Why Convert WebP to JPG?

The WebP image format has gained popularity due to its ability to provide high-quality images with smaller file sizes, making it ideal for web use. However, there are times when you may need to convert WebP to JPG. For instance, JPG is more universally supported across all devices and platforms, making it easier to share or edit. Additionally, some older software or devices may not recognize WebP files. That’s where the Image to WebP tool comes in handy.
