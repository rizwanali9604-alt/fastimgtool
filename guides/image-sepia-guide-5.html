What is Sepia Tone?

Sepia tone is a reddish-brown monochromatic effect often associated with old photographs. It mimics the look of early photographic prints, where images were chemically treated to preserve them and give them a distinct warm hue. Today, digital sepia filters allow you to recreate this effect instantly.
