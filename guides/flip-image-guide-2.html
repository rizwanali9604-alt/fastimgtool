Why Sharpen an Image?

Sharpening an image enhances its clarity and detail, making it look more appealing and professional. Here’s why you might want to sharpen your images:

  • Improve Focus: Corrects blurriness caused by camera shake or poor focus.
  • Enhance Details: Brings out finer details in the image, making it more engaging.
  • Boost Visual Appeal: Sharp images look cleaner and more polished, leaving a better impression.
  • Optimize for Print or Web: Sharpened images look better when printed or displayed online.

Tips for Sharpening Images Effectively

While Flip Image makes sharpening easy, these tips can help you achieve the best results:

  • Start Subtle: Avoid over-sharpening, as it can introduce noise and artifacts. Make small adjustments and preview the changes.
  • Focus on Key Areas: Sharpen the most important parts of the image, such as the subject’s face or intricate details.
  • Use High-Quality Originals: Starting with a high-resolution image ensures better results when sharpened.
  • Compare Before and After: Flip Image allows you to compare the original and sharpened versions side by side, helping you make precise adjustments.
