Tips for Effective Image Sharpening

While sharpening images online is simple, following these tips can help you achieve better results:

  • Avoid Over-Sharpening - Excessive sharpening can make images look unnatural and introduce artifacts. Use the slider in Image Sharpen to find the right balance.
  • Start with High-Quality Images - Sharpening works best on images that are already decent quality. Blurry or low-resolution images may not improve significantly.
  • Preview Before Downloading - Always check the preview to ensure the image looks sharp and natural. This step prevents unnecessary re-editing.
  • Experiment with Settings - Don’t hesitate to try different sharpening levels to see what works best for your specific image.
