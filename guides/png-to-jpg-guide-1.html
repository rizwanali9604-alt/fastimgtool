Why Convert JPG to PNG?

Before diving into the steps, let’s explore why you might need to convert JPG to PNG:

  • Transparency Support: PNG files support transparent backgrounds, unlike JPGs.
  • Lossless Compression: PNG retains image quality without compression artifacts.
  • Better for Text and Graphics: Ideal for logos, icons, and screenshots.
