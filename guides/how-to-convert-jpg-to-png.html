What is JPG to PNG Conversion?

At its core, converting a JPG (or JPEG) to a PNG is the process of changing an image file from one format to another. But this simple act has profound implications for the image's data, quality, and capabilities. It's like repackaging the contents of a cardboard box (JPG) into a durable, clear plastic container (PNG). The contents are the same pixels, but the new container offers better protection and new features.
