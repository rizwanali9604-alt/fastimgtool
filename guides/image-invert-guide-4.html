What Does Inverting Image Colors Do?

When you invert image colors, you reverse the color values of every pixel in the image. This means:

  • Black becomes white
  • White becomes black
  • Colors shift to their complementary hues (e.g., red becomes cyan)

This effect can create a dramatic, surreal, or high-contrast look, making it popular for creative projects, scientific imaging, and accessibility adjustments.
