Common mistakes to avoid

  • Starting from over-compressed sources: You cannot recover detail lost in a 30KB WhatsApp image. Always keep a full-resolution master and export marketplace copies with JPG to PNG.

  • Applying maximum effect settings: Sliders at 100% rarely look professional. Subtle adjustments between 10–30% usually outperform dramatic edits on product photography.
