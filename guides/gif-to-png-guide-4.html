Why Convert HEIC to JPG?

HEIC files offer superior quality and smaller file sizes compared to JPG, but they are not widely supported outside Apple’s ecosystem. Converting HEIC to JPG ensures compatibility with:

  • Windows devices
  • Social media platforms
  • Email attachments
  • Third-party editing software

Additionally, JPG is a standard format that works seamlessly across all devices and platforms, making it the go-to choice for sharing and storing images.
