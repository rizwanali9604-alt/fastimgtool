How to Use Image Resizer Online

How to Use Image Resizer Online

Follow these simple steps to resize your images quickly and easily:

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the "Upload" button or drag and drop your image file into the designated area.
  2. Select Resize Options: Choose between percentage scaling or entering specific width/height dimensions in pixels.
  3. Maintain Aspect Ratio (Optional): Check the "Keep Aspect Ratio" box to prevent distortion while resizing.
  4. Preview & Adjust: View the resized image preview and make adjustments if needed.
  5. Download Your Image: Click the "Download" button to save your resized image to your device.

For best results, use high-quality original images and check the file size after resizing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize an image online?

To resize an image online, simply upload your image to the Image Resizer Online tool, select your desired dimensions or percentage scaling, and click the "Resize" button. The tool will process your image and provide a downloadable link for the resized version.

What image formats are supported by the Image Resizer Online tool?

The Image Resizer Online tool supports a wide range of formats, including JPEG, PNG, BMP, GIF, and more. You can upload and resize images in any of these formats without any issues.

Is there a limit to the file size I can resize?

Yes, the Image Resizer Online tool typically has a file size limit, which is usually mentioned on the website. For most tools, the limit is around 5MB to 10MB per image. If your file exceeds this limit, consider compressing it before resizing.
