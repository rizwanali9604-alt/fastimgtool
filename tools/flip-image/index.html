How to Use Flip Image

How to Use Flip Image

Flipping an image is a simple yet powerful way to create a mirrored effect. Follow these easy steps to flip your image:

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the "Upload" button and select the image you want to flip from your device.
  2. Choose Flip Option: Select whether you want to flip the image horizontally or vertically using the provided buttons.
  3. Preview the Flip: Instantly see the flipped version of your image in the preview window.
  4. Download Your Image: Once satisfied, click the "Download" button to save the flipped image to your device.

It's that easy! Flip your images effortlessly with just a few clicks.

What does the Flip Image tool do?

The Flip Image tool allows you to flip your images horizontally or vertically. This means you can mirror your image along the horizontal or vertical axis, creating a reversed version of the original.

Is the Flip Image tool free to use?

Yes, the Flip Image tool is completely free to use. You can upload your image, flip it as needed, and download the result without any charges or subscriptions.

Does flipping an image affect its quality?

No, flipping an image does not affect its quality. The tool preserves the original resolution and clarity of the image, ensuring that the flipped version looks just as sharp as the original.
