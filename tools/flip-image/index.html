Flip Image

About Flip Image

Flip images horizontally or vertically instantly. Free online tool. This tool is completely free, works directly in your browser, and respects your privacy – no uploads, no tracking.

How to Use Flip Image

  1. Upload your image: Click the "Choose File" button and select an image from your device (JPG, PNG, WEBP supported).
  2. Preview: Your image will appear below the upload area. You can see exactly what you're working with.
  3. Download: Click the "Download" button. Your processed image will be saved instantly.

That's it – no registration, no hidden steps, no watermarks.

Why Use Flip Image?

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions, no credits, no hidden fees.
  • Private & Secure: All processing happens in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
  • Fast & Reliable: Optimized code ensures instant results, even for large files.
  • No File Size Limits: Process images as large as you need (within your browser's memory limits).
  • Works Offline: Once the page loads, you can even disconnect from the internet – the tool still works!

Common Use Cases for Flip Image

Our Flip Image tool is perfect for:

  • Preparing images for social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter).
  • Optimizing images for websites to improve loading speed.
  • Creating black‑and‑white photos for artistic projects.
  • Converting images to a uniform format for presentations or documents.
  • Reducing file size for email attachments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Flip Image really free?

Yes, completely free. There are no premium plans, no watermarks, and no limits on usage. We believe essential tools should be accessible to everyone.

Do you store my images?

No. All processing is done locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers – they stay on your device. This ensures complete privacy.

What image formats are supported?

Currently, we support JPG, PNG, and WEBP. We're constantly adding more formats based on user feedback.

Can I use this tool on mobile?

Absolutely. The tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets, though for very large images a desktop may perform better.

Why is my image not downloading?

If you encounter issues, try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge work best). Also, ensure you have enough free memory – very large images (over 50 MP) may cause problems.

How do you make money if the tool is free?

We display non‑intrusive ads to cover hosting and development costs. We never sell your data or require payment.
