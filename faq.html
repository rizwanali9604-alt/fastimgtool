Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tools really free?
Yes, all tools are completely free with no hidden fees. We support the site via non‑intrusive ads.
Do you store my images?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
What image formats are supported?
We currently support JPG, PNG, and WEBP. More formats will be added soon.
Is there a file size limit?
No, but very large images may be slow depending on your device's memory. We recommend images under 50 MB for best performance.
How do I contact support?
Visit our Contact page to send us an email. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
Can I use the tools on mobile?
Absolutely. The site is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.
