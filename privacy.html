Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Fast Image Tools, we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on‑going operation of one of our services.

Information We Collect

No image uploads: All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers – they stay on your device.

Cookies and advertising: We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here.

Log data: Like most websites, we collect limited log data (browser type, referring pages, date/time) to help us understand how the site is used. This data is anonymized.

Third‑Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically.
